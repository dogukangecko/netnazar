🧿 NetNazar — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Summary: We do not collect your data.

  • No data collection. NetNazar contains no analytics, ads or tracking. Nothing is sent to the developer.
  • Scan data stays on your device. Device information discovered on your local network (IP/MAC addresses, device names) is stored only on your phone/computer.
  • Remote mode is entirely yours. The optional remote monitoring feature connects only to a server you set up and operate. Data goes there and nowhere else — not to the developer or any third party.
  • Integrations talk to your endpoints. Optional notification channels (MQTT, webhook, Telegram, ntfy) send data only to addresses you configure.
  • Local Network permission is used solely to discover devices on your network; results never leave your device.
